Neighbourhood Statistics

 

The Neighbourhood Statistics website is part of the ONS. It has over 550 datasets across ten different subjects.

Neighbourhood data can be browsed directly and also accessed electronically using the API. Searching on this page will bring back a sample directly from the NeSS website.

You can download this data and find more (including more guidance on accessing the data) on the Neighbourhood Statistics website .

The Neighbourhood Statistics Website is a free to access online data resource. It contains datasets that describe the characteristics of a neighbourhood, with a particular focus on deprivation. The website includes results from the 2001 and 2011 Census and other official government statistics. There are two primary routes to the data: > Neighbourhood Summary provides an overview of your local area > Detailed Route - contains in depth statistics for a specific geographic area
