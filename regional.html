Regional Statistics

Share on Twitter Share on Facebook Share on Google Plus

In 2011 ONS replaced all compendium titles, including Regional Trends, by a policy of publishing information on the website, as soon as it is ready. The Regional Trends tables were published as supporting data for Region and Country Profiles. These profiles are organised into four broad topics: Population and Migration, Economy, Social Indicators, and Environment, plus Key Statistics. See more

These combine the contents of Regional Snapshot on the old ONS website with the Region and Country Profiles from Regional Trends.

Following a review in 2013 of non-statutory statistical products to identify reductions to statistical outputs in order to make cost savings, a decision was made in August 2014 to discontinue the production of the Regional and Country Profiles. No updates to these charts and tables are therefore planned, and the last updates were made in December 2013:
