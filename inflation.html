Statistical Bulletin

Consumer Price Index, February 2014


1.9% growth in Consume Price Index (CPI)in the years to January 2014, down fom 2.0% in Deember 2013


Producer Price Index, February 2014


The output price index for goods produced by UK manufacturers (factory gate prices) rose 0.5% in the year to February.


Crime Statistics, period ending September 2013


The police recorded 3.7 million offences in the year ending September 2013, a decrease of 3 % compared with the previous year.


Second Estimate of GDP, Q4 2013


UK gross domestic product (GDP) in volume terms was estimated to have increased by 0.7% between Q3 2013 and Q4 2013.


Retail Sales, February 2014


Year-on-year estimates of the quantity bought in the retail industry continued to show growth.


Quarterly National Accounts, Q4 2013


The households’ saving ratio was estimated to be 5.1% in 2013 compared with 7.3% in 2012.
